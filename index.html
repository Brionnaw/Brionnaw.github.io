About Me

I'm a software engineer who has experience in JavaScript programming based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I enjoy creating clean, intuitive and responsive applications to provide the best user experiences. You can catch me exploring new frameworks, taking selfies, or snoozing the day away.

I started designing and developing a passion for web design at a young age and some of my design skills and knowledge are self taught. I decided to explore more into progamming and immerse myself into learning web development. I found very quickly that i had a knack for programming and even though there are few black women in tech, it only makes me a better engineer and critical thinker.

I'm currently looking for opportunites to work and contribute ideas and skills on a team. Feel free to contact me for a skype call or coffee .
