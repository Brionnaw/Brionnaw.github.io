About Me

I'm a web developer who has a background in full stack JavaScript based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I spend eight to ten hours a day building, improving, and debugging web applications using MEAN Stack. As a full stack developer, I enjoy creative problem solving, exploring new frameworks, and thinking about how the front end and back end are communicating with each other.

I started designing and developing a passion for web and graphic design at a young age and some of my design skills and knowledge are self taught. I decided to explore more into coding and immerse myself into learning web development. I found very quickly that coding was my niche and even though there a few black women in tech, it only makes me a better engineer and critical thinker.

My purpose as a web developer is to assist and offer the highest quality service of professionally built websites. I enjoy creating clean, intuitive and responsive applications to provide the best user experiences.
